# Meeting Coverage Checklist

Systematic checklist for processing government meeting documents — before, during, and after the meeting event.

---

## Pre-meeting (agenda review)

**Identify the body and meeting type**
- [ ] Which governing body? (City Council, Planning Commission, School Board, etc.)
- [ ] Regular meeting or special meeting? (Special meetings have restricted agenda scope)
- [ ] Quorum present? (If listed on agenda — absence of a member may affect outcomes)

**Scan the agenda structure**
- [ ] How many items are on the consent calendar?
- [ ] Are any items labeled "urgency" or "emergency"?
- [ ] Are any public hearings scheduled?
- [ ] Are there any items listed as "continued from" a prior meeting?
- [ ] Has a supplemental or amended agenda been posted? (Items added late lack the normal notice period)

**Flag agenda items for priority review**
- [ ] Any item with a fiscal note above $100,000
- [ ] Any item involving a waiver of competitive bidding, environmental review, or public notice
- [ ] Any item amending a prior ordinance, resolution, or contract
- [ ] Any item involving a permit or entitlement after a public hearing
- [ ] Any item that appeared on a prior agenda but was continued or tabled
- [ ] Any consent item whose description includes "sole source," "emergency," or "amendment"

**Gather supporting materials**
- [ ] Staff reports for all non-consent items
- [ ] Prior meeting minutes referenced in the current agenda
- [ ] Any ordinance or resolution text attached to the agenda

---

## During/processing the meeting record

**Establish the context**
- [ ] Date and time of meeting recorded
- [ ] Attending members recorded (absent members affect quorum and majority calculations)
- [ ] Whether a quorum was present throughout (some items may have been acted on without quorum — flag this)

**For each consent calendar action**
- [ ] List of items approved as a block
- [ ] Any items pulled from consent for separate discussion — flag these
- [ ] Vote on consent block (unanimous? Any nay votes or abstentions?)

**For each substantive agenda item**
- [ ] Who presented? (Staff, applicant, outside consultant)
- [ ] Was public comment allowed? How many speakers? Were they in support or opposition?
- [ ] What was the motion made? (Exact language matters for ordinances and resolutions)
- [ ] Who seconded?
- [ ] Were there amendments to the motion? (Each amendment is its own vote)
- [ ] Final vote tally: who voted aye, who voted nay, who abstained, who was absent
- [ ] Was the outcome consistent with staff's recommendation?

**Closed session**
- [ ] Was closed session held? What was the stated purpose? (Must be announced in open session)
- [ ] What was reported out of closed session in open session?
- [ ] Any action taken in closed session? (Settlements, personnel actions, real property direction)

**Procedural anomalies to flag**
- [ ] Any point of order raised?
- [ ] Any vote challenged or reconsidered?
- [ ] Any member recuse themselves (conflict of interest)?
- [ ] Any item acted on without adequate public notice?
- [ ] Meeting adjourned to a continuation? (Sets time and place for resumption)

---

## Post-meeting (document finalization)

**After the meeting, confirm**
- [ ] Approved resolutions and ordinances are dated and numbered consistently with the minutes
- [ ] Any ordinance passed is noted as having a 30-day effective date (or urgency if immediate)
- [ ] Any contract authorized is being tracked against the executed contract document (to appear later)
- [ ] Any item continued or tabled is noted in the monitoring list for future meetings
- [ ] Approved minutes match the draft minutes (when approved at next meeting)

**Items to carry forward**
- [ ] Any item continued to a future meeting: what date? What condition triggers its return?
- [ ] Any item referred to a committee or sub-body: when does it return?
- [ ] Any direction given to staff (return with a report by X date, negotiate terms and return, etc.)
- [ ] Any flag items from this meeting that need cross-reference with the budget, prior contracts, or entity registry
