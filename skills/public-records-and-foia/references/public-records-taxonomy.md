# Public Records Taxonomy

Record types by government body, with access requirements and notes on what is typically available without a formal request.

---

## City / municipality

| Record type | Typical availability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Council meeting agendas | Posted publicly, no request | 72-hour advance posting required in most states |
| Council meeting minutes | Posted after approval, no request | May have a delay of 2-4 weeks before formal approval |
| Adopted ordinances | Posted after adoption, no request | Numbered; searchable in most modern city systems |
| Adopted resolutions | Posted after adoption, no request | May be in agenda packet or separate |
| Staff reports / agenda packets | Posted with agenda, no request | May be revised before the meeting; note which version |
| Adopted budget | Posted after adoption, no request | Proposals typically posted when transmitted to council |
| Contracts above threshold | May require request; sometimes posted | Threshold varies by city ($10K–$100K typical for online posting) |
| Contracts below threshold | Request typically required | Part of city's record-keeping obligations |
| Invoices and payment records | Request required | May be in financial management system; open data portals in some cities |
| City payroll / salary data | Request required; aggregate often posted | Individual employee salary is public in most states |
| Personnel files | Request required; significant exemptions | Disciplinary records may be available; performance evaluations often exempt |
| Building permits | Posted or searchable in permit portal | Usually searchable by address online |
| Code enforcement actions | Request required; some online portals | Violation notices, inspection reports |
| City email correspondence | Request required | Subject to exemptions; often the subject of investigative requests |
| Attorney-client communications | Generally exempt | Related to pending litigation |
| Closed session minutes | Generally exempt | Some states require release after matter concludes |
| Real property negotiations | Exempt while pending; public after | Cities negotiate land purchases in private |

## School district / educational agency

| Record type | Typical availability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Board meeting agendas and minutes | Posted publicly, no request | Same open meetings requirements as other bodies |
| Adopted budget | Posted after adoption, no request | May include multiple budgets (general, special purpose) |
| Annual audit | Posted after completion, no request | Due to state education agency on a schedule |
| Collective bargaining agreements | Posted after ratification, no request | Current contracts typically on district website |
| Salary schedules | Posted, no request | Ranges by position and step; individual salaries may require request |
| Enrollment and demographic data | Posted, no request (state-reported) | Annual reporting requirements |
| Student discipline data (aggregate) | Posted, no request | Cannot identify individual students |
| Individual student records | Exempt (FERPA) | Protected federal privacy law; not available to press |
| Teacher credentials and certifications | Request; some states have online lookup | Public employee; credential status is public |
| Special education program data | Aggregate data public; individual exempt | FERPA protects individual student records |

## County / regional

| Record type | Typical availability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Board of supervisors agendas and minutes | Posted publicly, no request | Same requirements as cities |
| Property tax rolls | Public; online assessor search | Current assessed values, ownership records |
| Recorded documents (deeds, liens, mortgages) | Public; recorder's office | Available by property address or grantor/grantee name |
| Probate and estate records | Public; court records | Available at courthouse |
| Superior court civil case records | Public, with exceptions | Online access varies by county |
| Jail booking records | Public in most states | Recent bookings often posted daily |
| County health department inspections | Request; some online portals | Restaurant inspections often online |
| Campaign finance filings (county elections) | Posted online | State and local campaign finance portals |
| Lobbying disclosure | Request; some online | Varies significantly by jurisdiction |

## Special districts (water, fire, transit, sanitation, etc.)

Special districts vary significantly by enabling statute and jurisdiction. At minimum:

| Record type | Typical availability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Board meeting records | Posted publicly; same open meetings rules | May have less robust website infrastructure |
| Budget and financial reports | Posted or available on request | Less consistent than cities in posting practice |
| Rate-setting documents | Posted when rate hearings scheduled | Rate increases require notice and hearing |
| Grant records | Available; may require request | Federal grants traceable via USASpending.gov |

**Special district caveat**: special districts often have smaller staff, less sophisticated records management, and less media scrutiny than cities or counties. Mandatory disclosures may be less consistently available even when legally required. A missing document from a special district may reflect capacity constraints rather than intent — but the legal obligation is the same.

## Federal records relevant to local government coverage

| Source | What it contains | Access |
|---|---|---|
| USASpending.gov | All federal contracts, grants, loans by recipient | Public, searchable online |
| SAM.gov | Contractor registrations, debarment actions | Public, searchable online |
| FEMA flood maps and grants | Flood zone maps; disaster grant recipients | Public online |
| HUD CPD Maps | CDBG and other HUD program expenditures by jurisdiction | Public online |
| FCC license database | Broadcast licenses, cell tower permits | Public, searchable online |
| EPA compliance data | Environmental violations and enforcement actions | Public via ECHO database |
| OSHA inspection data | Workplace safety inspections and violations | Public via OSHA website |
| FAA records | Aircraft registrations, pilot certificates | Public |
| SEC EDGAR | Financial disclosures for public companies (relevant if a public company is a vendor) | Public, searchable online |

## State-level records relevant to local government

| Record type | Where to look |
|---|---|
| Business registrations (corporations, LLCs) | Secretary of State |
| Professional licenses (contractors, engineers, architects) | State licensing board |
| Charitable organization registrations | State attorney general or charity bureau |
| State campaign finance | Secretary of State or elections division |
| State court records | Statewide court case search (varies by state) |
| State grant awards to local agencies | State budget office or grantor agency |
| Workers' compensation claims (public employees) | State workers' comp board; varies |
| State financial reports for local agencies | State controller or auditor |
